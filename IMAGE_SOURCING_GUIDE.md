## License Information

**Important:** Make sure any image you download has one of these licenses:

✅ **Royalty-Free** - Use indefinitely
✅ **Creative Commons CC0** - Public domain, use freely
✅ **Commercial License** - Purchased license for commercial use

❌ **Creative Commons with Attribution** - Requires credit
❌ **Editorial Only** - Cannot use for commercial products
❌ **Rights Reserved** - Cannot use without permission
